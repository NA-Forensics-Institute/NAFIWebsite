General Info

What?

North Allegheny Forensics Institute is a week-long summer camp geared towards North Allegheny students interested in learning about speech and debate. Run by the most experienced and successful members of the North Allegheny Speech and Debate team, campers will be given an elite opportunity to enhance their public speaking, research, and argumentive skills in their choice of a forensics event.

When?

NAFI will be held the week of August 19-23rd, 9:00 AM - 3:30 PM.

Where?

All sessions will be held at North Allegheny Senior High School.

10375 Perry Hwy, Wexford, PA 15090

NAFI is available to exclusively North Allegheny students, entering grades 6-11 in the 19-20 year.
